Production Process

Your Report Is Being Built

Every Revenue Intelligence Brief™ is built from scratch using your Client Blueprint™, current market intelligence, and proprietary scoring. Here is what happens between receiving your Blueprint and delivering your Brief.

1

Client Blueprint™ Received

Your strategic profile is confirmed and research parameters are set.

2

Market Research Underway

We begin identifying projects, market activity, and opportunities in your target geography.

3

Opportunity Analysis in Progress

The ConstructIQ Intelligence Engine™ evaluates fit, timing, readiness, and revenue potential.

4

Decision-Maker Research

We identify and verify the right contacts for each qualified opportunity.

5

Intelligence Scoring

Every opportunity receives a ConstructIQ Intelligence Score™ based on your Blueprint.

6

Executive Report Preparation

Your highest-priority opportunities are organized into your Revenue Intelligence Brief™.

7

Revenue Intelligence Brief™ Ready

Your Brief is reviewed and delivered to reports@constructiqpb.com.

Typical delivery is 3–5 business days after your completed Client Blueprint™ is received. You will receive an email from reports@constructiqpb.com when your Brief is ready.
