The Platform

Not lead generation.
Construction intelligence.

ConstructIQ is not a generic lead generation service. It is a construction intelligence platform that combines public-record research, permit tracking, market signals, decision-maker verification, and proprietary scoring logic to identify business development opportunities aligned with each client’s buy box.

Opportunity Intelligence

We track permits, public records, entitlement filings, RFPs, and market signals across your target geography to surface opportunities at the right stage — before they hit mainstream channels.

Decision Maker Research

Beyond the permit, we identify the right people — owners, developers, project leads, capital partners — with verified contact information and the context you need to reach them effectively.

Outreach Strategy

Each brief includes a recommended outreach strategy and AI-generated email copy tailored to the opportunity — so your team enters every conversation with the right message, at the right moment.
