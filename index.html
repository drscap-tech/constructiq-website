ConstructIQ combines public-record research, permit tracking, planning activity, market signals, developer activity, decision-maker verification, and proprietary scoring to identify opportunities aligned with each client’s Client Blueprint™.

ConstructIQ Intelligence Score™

Every opportunity in your Revenue Intelligence Brief™ receives a ConstructIQ Intelligence Score™ (CIS™) — a proprietary composite score that evaluates fit, timing, readiness, and strategic alignment to your Client Blueprint™.

The CIS™ tells your team exactly which opportunities deserve immediate attention and which to monitor — so your business development effort is always focused where it matters most.

ConstructIQ does not publish scoring weights or methodology. The Score reflects a multi-factor proprietary model. Inputs vary by opportunity type, market, and client Blueprint.
