Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026  ·  Version: Draft 1.0 (Pending Review)  ·  Last Updated: July 2026

1. Overview

ConstructIQ™ ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients, prospects, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name, company name, and business email address
  • Company website and business type
  • Target market, opportunity preferences, and project size criteria
  • Client Blueprint™ data — your business strategy, goals, exclusions, and preferences
  • Messages and inquiries submitted through the website or by email

We also collect certain technical information automatically, including IP address, browser type, referring URL, and pages visited, via Google Analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Produce your Revenue Intelligence Brief™ and related deliverables
  • Communicate with you about your order, report status, and account
  • Send status emails related to your report production
  • Improve our research methodology and platform
  • Respond to support and billing inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or business strategy data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Client Blueprint™ Confidentiality

Your Client Blueprint™ — including your target markets, strategic goals, competitive advantages, and exclusions — is treated as strictly confidential. This data is used solely to produce your intelligence deliverables and is never shared with other clients or third parties beyond what is required to operate the service.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Square — payment processing
  • Google Analytics — anonymous website usage analytics
  • Supabase — secure database and authentication (Sprint 2+)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (Sprint 2+)
  • Formspree — contact form submission handling (interim)

We do not share identifiable client data with AI providers beyond what is necessary to produce your deliverable, and we do not use client data to train public AI models.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and server-side storage of all sensitive data. API keys and credentials are never stored in client-facing code.

7. Data Retention

We retain client records and report case files for a minimum of three years to support audit, quality review, and client service. You may request deletion of your personal data by contacting support@constructiqpb.com. Certain data may be retained as required by law or legitimate business interest.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact support@constructiqpb.com.

9. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics (Google Analytics) and session management. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

10. Children's Privacy

ConstructIQ services are intended for business professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the Services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

Data requests and account support: support@constructiqpb.com
